> Heads up! This guide uses an experimental feature of SANITY.

Did you know that you can give your client a staging environment with SANITY.io? Even on their free plan?

Staging environments empower content editors to test major content revisions without fear of breaking the main production website. A virtual content "sandbox" to play in.

To set this up we will be using the [Spaces API](https://www.sanity.io/docs/spaces) inside of SANITY studio, a subdomain, and a staging branch of your website. I have done this with SANITY, Gatsby and Netlify however the core ideas presented here should translate to most major hosting and framework combinations.
